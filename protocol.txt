The Definition of the protocol used for UDP communication.

From the streamer to the receiver (Port 8051):

- captured timestamp

- captured pose of camera

- color jpeg image data

- depth data (16 bit) PNG data (0 is error/infinite/unknown, and otherwise in millimeters.)



From the VR receiver to the streamer (PORT 8052):

- timestamp of pose

- pose data (6DoF)

- Field of View (in future)

- IPD (in future)
